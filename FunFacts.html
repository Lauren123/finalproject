Fun Facts

 

For centuries diabetes was diagnosed by the doctor dipping his finger into a urine sample and tasting it to detect abnormal sugar levels.

   

More than 3 million people in the UK are diagnosed with diabetes

  

World Diabetes Day is November 14th!                                                                               Diabetes does NOT have to stand in the way of your dreams!

  

Physical activity is good for everyone. Being active is really important if you have diabetes.

  

Many celebrities and have diabetes. Such as: Actors Nick Jonas and Tom Hanks and Christopher Biggins, Singer Amelia Lily and Top English Rugby sportsman Chris Pennell!.

   

Diabetes means that your blood glucose, also called blood sugar, is too high. Glucose comes from the food you eat and is needed to fuel our bodies. Glucose is also stored in our liver and muscles.

  

Humans are not the only species that can suffer from diabetes. Animals can have diabetes too.

  

  

Quiz Time!!

Where is the best place to get information on diabetes?

a) your friends

b) the internet

c) your doctor

d) your neighbour

Did you guess c? That's right! You might be able to get information from all of those places but some of it could be wrong or dangerous. It is important that you get the correct and best advice possible to allow you to manage your diabetes in the best way. Getting advice from your parents or a doctor will make sure that you have not discovered any wrong information or anything that is too good to be true.

Does having Diabetes mean that you can't eat sweets anymore?

a) yes

b) no

Did you guess b? That's right! Children with diabtes can still eat certain sweets as long as they do so as part of a healthy diet. Like everyone else, a person with diabetes shouldn't eat too many sweets because they are high in calories and they don't have many vitamins and minerals.

True or False, Exercise is good for children with diabetes?

a) True

b) False

Did you guess a? That's right! Staying active is important for all children whether they have diabetes or not. Exercise can help you to stay a healthy weight and helps your muscles to burn glucose. This reduces your blood sugar levels and increases the effectiveness of insulin in your body.

Can you catch diabetes from another person?

a) Yes

b) No

Did you guess b? That's right! Diabetes is not contagious. This means it is not possible to catch it from another person.

True or false, everyone that has diabetes must take insulin?

a) True

b) False

Did you guess b? That's right! Anyone who has type 1 diabetes needs to take insulin injections because their pancreases don't make insulin anymore. Some children (but not everyone) who has type 2 diabetes need insulin to manage their blood sugar levels.
